About LabAutomation

LabAutomation is a platform for electronic purchase and electronic labwork of throughout the year in Pakistan. Due to COVID-19 ( Coronavirus ), it is necessary that people can buy electronic purchase with ease from their home with much less effort. So basic purpose of this platform is to ease the process of buying and selling of electronic purchase and electronic labwork for people of Pakistan and throughout the world.


LabAutomation was initially launched in 2016 to cater the needs of electronic purchase and electronic labwork in pakistan. But later on, it is re-launched in the 2020 to cater bigger needs of the community including electronicians, resistors, gears, capacitors, fuses, etc through the year in the era of COVID-19.
